Rates and Packages

Fishing Charter

  • Half–Day Charters (4 hours): $740.00 - Maximum of 5 guests.
  • Full–Day Charters (8 hours): $1400.00 - Maximum of 5 guests.
  • All Day Charter (10 hours): $1650.00 - Maximum of 5 guests.

Note about our Offerings:

  • Hourly rates increase by $20.00/hour for a 6-person charter. Maximum 6 people.
  • A Fuel surcharge may apply for long distance trips.
  • All prices are in Canadian Dollars and do not include 5% GST

Groups of 3-4 per boat is optimal for comfort, room to fish, and rod time. Gratuities are optional but always appreciated!

All fishing charters include:

  • Fuel
  • Bait (if required)
  • Safety gear
  • Fishing gear (rods, reels, tackle)
  • Boat and captain

Guest must supply:

  • Appropriate clothing for weather conditions
  • Sunscreen
  • Food and beverages
  • Valid fishing license from Fisheries and Oceans Canada

Release of Cremains - Returning to the Sea with Honor, Led by Kwigwis

Flowers are often tossed into the water (provided by customer) after which the vessel encircles the area and returns to port. No extra charge to scatter multiple ashes when booking with passengers.
  • Captain only scatters ashes: $250.00 We will take pictures and provide GPS marker on the map where the cremains are scattered. Arrangements can also be made to pick up ashes from a local funeral home, or any 3rd party if you wish.
